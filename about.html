What do we do?

At Oracle Recruitment we provide cost effective, efficient and a customised recruitment services to all businesses and deliver the highest standard of quality our clients expect. We facilitate permanent, contract and temporary roles for all industries.
Based in Kildare, our success is based on partnerships with our clients by providing them with innovative solutions and cost-effective options brought about by our strong management, technical excellence and reliable judgment.

How do We do it?

Matching the right candidate to the right organisation is our number one objective when we partner with a client. We do not believe in cutting corners and use every available resource we have available to us to ensure we identify the perfect talent to suit your business.
Recruitment is not just about filling an empty vacancy, it’s about connecting the right person, to the right job in the right organisation.
